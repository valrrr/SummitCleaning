Common Questions

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No — most clients provide entry instructions and go about their day. Our teams are vetted and professional so you can book with complete confidence.

How does pricing work?

Pricing is based on your home's square footage and service type. You'll see your exact price on the booking form before entering any payment information — no quotes required.

What if I'm not satisfied?

If anything falls short, let us know within 24 hours and we'll return to make it right at no charge — no questions asked.

Will I get the same team every time?

Yes — recurring clients are matched with a consistent team. You'll always know who's coming through your door.

Do you clean Airbnb properties?

Yes — same-day turnaround cleaning for Airbnb and STR properties across the South Bay. Fast, reliable, photo-ready standard every time.
