Who We Are

A local company built for this community

Summit Residence Co. is a luxury residential and Airbnb cleaning service owned and operated in the South Bay Area. We were founded with one purpose — to bring a higher standard of home care to Saratoga, Los Gatos, and Almaden Valley.

We are not a national franchise. We are not a staffing app. We are a local business that takes personal accountability for every clean we schedule.

Every team in our network is vetted and background-verified before their first job. Recurring clients receive the same trusted team on every appointment — because consistency is not a bonus, it is the standard.
